# FOCUCHRIST.COM CONTENT GUIDELINES
## Our Moral Code & Values

*Created: March 17, 2026*

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

**Jesus Christ is the center of all things.**

The name "focusChrist" says it all — everything points to Him.

---

## OUR BELIEFS

### We Believe:
- Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Savior and Redeemer
- The Church of Jesus Christ has been restored through Joseph Smith
- The Book of Mormon is another testament of Jesus Christ
- The Bible, Book of Mormon, Doctrine & Covenants, and Pearl of Great Price all testify of Christ

### We Respect:
- All religions are important
- People of all faiths are seeking truth
- Jesus Christ is found in many paths
- Our way is not the only way — but it is our way

---

## WHAT WE DON'T DO

- ❌ Make fun of other religions
- ❌ Criticize other faiths
- ❌ Claim our way is the only way
- ❌ Put down other denominations or beliefs

---

## WHAT WE DO

- ✅ Share our faith with love
- ✅ Answer questions about LDS beliefs
- ✅ Testify of Jesus Christ
- ✅ Respect all seekers
- ✅ Invite, never condemn

---

## CONTENT RULES

### Tone:
- Warm, inviting, loving
- "Come, follow Christ" — not "We're right, you're wrong"
- Testify, don't argue
- Share, don't push

### Examples:
- ✅ "We believe..." — not "Only we believe..."
- ✅ "This is what helps us..." — not "This is the only truth..."
- ✅ "We invite you to learn more..." — not "You're wrong if you don't..."

---

## LDS MORAL CODE

Guiding everything we do:

1. **Love** — Christlike love for all
2. **Respect** — Honor other beliefs
3. **Honesty** — Truthful, accurate
4. **Humility** — Not superior, just grateful
5. **Invitation** — Always invite, never force
