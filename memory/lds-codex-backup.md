# THEME 1: THE RESTORATION & JOSEPH SMITH

## Joseph Smith (1830-1844) — The Prophet

### First Vision
- God the Father and Jesus Christ appeared to Joseph in a Sacred Grove in 1820
- "I saw two Personages, whose brightness and glory defy all description"
- The First Vision is the foundational event of the Restoration

### The Book of Mormon
- "Another testament of Jesus Christ"
- Translated by the gift and power of God
- Moroni 10:4-5 — promise of personal revelation to know its truth

### Priesthood Restoration
- John the Baptist restored Aaronic Priesthood (May 15, 1829)
- Peter, James, and John restored Melchizedek Priesthood
- Priesthood authority returned to earth

### Key Teachings:
- **The Book of Mormon is the "keystone" of our religion**
- "A man must get the testimony of Jesus and have the spirit of prophecy to be a king and priest"
- "Happiness is the object and design of our existence"
- "I teach them correct principles and they govern themselves"
- The nature of God: "God himself was once as we are now"
- "The Father has a body of flesh and bones as tangible as man's"
- All religions have "an element of truth"
- "When the Prophet speaks... the debate is over"

### D&C Sections by Joseph:
- D&C 1: "The voice of warning"
- D&C 19: The infinite atonement
- D&C 76: The vision of the degrees of glory
- D&C 121: "The rights of the priesthood are inseparably connected with the powers of heaven"
- D&C 130: "When the Savior appears... we shall see as we are seen"
