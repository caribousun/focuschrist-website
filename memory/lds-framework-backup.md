## Core Doctrines (Always Hold True)

### The Godhead
- **God the Father** — Perfect, loving, wants us to return to Him
- **Jesus Christ** — Our Savior, Atonement makes redemption possible
- **Holy Ghost** — Comforter, teacher, reveals truth

### Plan of Salvation
- We lived with God before this life
- This life is a test — choose faith, service, love
- Death is not the end — resurrection and judgment follow
- Return to God's presence is the goal

### The Atonement
- Christ atoned for our sins, pain, and weaknesses
- Through Him, we can be cleansed and strengthened
- "He will take upon him death, that he may loose the bands of death" — 2 Nephi 9:12
- Key truth: We don't have to do this alone

### Faith & Works
- Faith *plus* works = salvation
- Not faith *instead of* works
- James 2:17 — "Faith without works is dead"
- Service is a manifestation of faith

### Agency
- God will not force us to choose right
- We must choose for ourselves
- Consequences follow choices
- This is why we can be tested

### Revelation
- God speaks to each of us personally
- Through prayer, the Spirit, scriptures, and leaders
- "The Holy Ghost shall be thy constant companion" — D&C 121:46

## Key Scriptures Wyatt Connects With

### D&C 6:36 — Wyatt's Anchor Verse
> "Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not."

**Context:** Christ is speaking. Simple, direct command. Don't look to the world, to people, to circumstances — look to Him.

**Application:**
- When Wyatt feels distant from God, this is a reminder to *turn toward* Him
- Doubt and fear are the enemies to push through
- God is the source of peace and direction

### D&C 121:34-46 — The Promise of Revelation

**Key promise:** "The Holy Ghost shall be thy constant companion"

### 2 Nephi 2:25 — The Purpose of Adversity
> "Adam fell that men might be; and men are that they might have joy."

**Key insight:** Adversity isn't punishment — it's part of the plan for growth and joy.

### Mosiah 2:17 — True Service

**This is why Wyatt lights up serving people.** It's literally worship.

### Alma 32:21 — Faith is a Hope
